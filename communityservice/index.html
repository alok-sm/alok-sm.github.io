Community Service

I strongly believe that the application of technology can alleviate several problems we face and my contributions to the community has been through the application of my technical skills toward complex problems

Content and licensing management Children’s Lovecastle Trust(CLT)


CLT is an NGO developing E-Learning content for primary and high school grades. My project was to design and develop an Android and Windows based content distribution and licensing solution which serves to make the distribution of E-learning content to Schools across India easier and prevent plagiarism of the content produced by CLT. My work has reached the lives of over 85,000 children and 4000 teachers (source).

Architecting E-education curriculum for a tribal school in Kerala

I was a part of the team that helped build the e-education curriculum for a remote school for orphaned tribal children in Kerala via the wayanad Project. Our team spent about a week in the school in order to assess the facilities in the school and to understand the target audience of the syllabus - the children. We used several open resources like Khan Academy to enable the underprivileged children to reap the benefits of modern technology in their education.
